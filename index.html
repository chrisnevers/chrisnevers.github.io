education

I received my bachelors in computer science from UMass Lowell in May 2019. My concentration is in compiler construction, for which I completed two directed studies under Jay McCarthy. For my project sequence, I implemented a compiler for a statically typed language to x86 assembly. Some of the features include:

  • Bytecode generation and JIT compiler*
  • Hygienic macros
  • Parametric polymorphism
  • Closures and Functions
  • Algebraic data types
  • Register allocation
  • Garbage Collection (stop and copy)
  • Vectors
  • Arrays
  • Control Flow (If-Else, While, For)
  • Standard library

* - still under development
